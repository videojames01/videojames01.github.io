Why ICT Suits Me

I like to think that ICT is a suitable field for me. In school my maths marks were average, but I've always excelled at languages. I know that ICT is a very maths-based study, but in my opinion it also has a lot to do with language. So I hope my natural affinity for languages helps me for the next four years.
